Hello, I'm Michael and I enjoy building production quality web applications that deliver an enjoyable experience for users. Welcome to my page. Please explore the site to get a better understanding of who I am and what skills I can bring to the table.

I typed my first JavaScript in February.  Since then, I’ve found a new passion in my life. This spring, I had the opportunity to be enrolled in The Iron Yard - Charlotte's first cohort, completing a rigorous and immersive 12-week course on JavaScript and MVC frameworks. Prior to my experience at The Iron Yard, I could barely explain the difference between classes and IDs used in HTML5 but now I am creating web apps using AngularJS.

As a developer, I am self motivated to do what it takes to complete any project I am working on. I enjoy collaborating on bigger projects with others to remain open minded, inspired and challenged to constantly grow and create. By attending local web focused Meetups I have been able to learn more about the field by emulating the more experienced developers around me. The community of developers is just one aspect of web development that I enjoy. The helpfulness and collaborative mindset is what drives me to give back and contribute to the open-source community.
